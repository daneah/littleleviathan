About

Dane Hillard is a man of many interests, of which music has been one of the most enduring. His tastes have changed dramatically over the years, but the album Little Leviathan is a snapshot of where his tastes have settled in recent years. Inspired by folk and indie singer/songwriters such as Iron & Wine, The Shins, Alexi Murdoch, and more, Little Leviathan is a collection of work about love, loss, and finding meaning in both.
